About

What MacroLogic does

MacroLogic estimates calorie and macro targets using your body stats, training frequency, daily movement, steps, and goal. It is designed to be faster than a full questionnaire, while still being more realistic than a basic one-size-fits-all calculator.

Results are for informational purposes only and should be adjusted based on progress, recovery, performance, and professional guidance where needed.
