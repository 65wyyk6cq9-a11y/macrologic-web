About

What MacroLogic does

MacroLogic estimates your daily calorie and macronutrient targets using your body stats, activity level, and goal. It is designed to give you a clean starting point for planning meals, tracking food, and reviewing progress.

The results are estimates for informational use only. They should be adjusted based on your real-world progress and, where appropriate, guidance from a qualified professional.
