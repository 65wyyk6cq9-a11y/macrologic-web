Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information we collect

MacroLogic is designed to minimize data collection. Information entered into the calculator, such as age, height, weight, sex, activity level, and goal, is processed locally in the browser and is not stored on our servers by this version of the app.

2. Analytics

If anonymous website analytics are added, they may collect limited usage information such as browser type, page views, or device category to help improve the service. No personally identifying information is intentionally collected through the calculator itself.

3. Cookies

MacroLogic may use cookies or similar technologies for site functionality, performance, or analytics if those features are enabled in the future.

4. Worldwide users

MacroLogic may be accessed from different countries. Users are responsible for understanding any local privacy rights or obligations that apply to them. Where applicable, local mandatory rights remain unaffected.

5. Third-party services

If third-party services are used in the future, such as analytics, hosting, or payment tools, they may process limited technical information as required to operate those services.

6. Data minimization

MacroLogic aims to collect as little personal data as possible. If user accounts, saved profiles, subscriptions, or email signups are added later, this policy should be updated before those features go live.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, use a public support contact you control, such as support@macrologic.app, once your domain and email are set up.
